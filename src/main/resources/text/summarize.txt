FinTech Plus Sync reported a strong Q2 2023, with revenues of 125 million, marking a 25% YoY increase. The company's gross profit margin was 58% and EBITDA increased to 37.5 million, corresponding to a 30% EBITDA margin. Net income rose to 16 million, up significantly from Q2 2022's 10 million. The expansion of high yield savings products and the new RoboAdvisor platform contributed to a substantial growth in the total addressable market. The company diversified its asset-backed securities portfolio and invested 25 million in AAA-rated corporate bonds. Total assets were 1.5 billion, total liabilities 900 million, and equity base 600 million. The company reported a healthy debt-to-equity ratio of 1.5 and impressive LTVCAC ratio of 3.5%. A value-at-risk model was adopted for risk management, while the company also reported a tier-one capital ratio of 12.5%. The Q3 forecast is positive, with revenues projected around 135 million, driven by blockchain solutions and AI-driven predictive analytics. Also, the company's fintech subsidiary, Pay Plus, is expected to raise 200 million in an upcoming IPO.
